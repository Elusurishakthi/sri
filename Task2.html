Different dancing styles of india

Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest and most popular classical dance forms of India.

Kathak

Kathak is a North Indian classical dance form that combines storytelling with rhythmic footwork and spins.

Odissi

Odissi is a classical dance form that originated in the state of Odisha in eastern India.

Manipuri

Manipuri is a classical dance form that originated in the northeastern state of Manipur.

Kuchipudi

Kuchipudi is a classical dance form that originated in the state of Andhra Pradesh in southern India.

Mohiniyattam

Mohiniyattam is a classical dance form that originated in the state of Kerala in southern India.

Kathakali

Kathakali is a traditional dance-drama form that originated in the state of Kerala in southern India.

Sattriya

Sattriya is a classical dance form that originated in the state of Assam in northeastern India.

Garba

Garba is a traditional folk dance form that originated in the state of Gujarat in western India.
